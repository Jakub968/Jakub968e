PVA Mesh system

diameter 15mm - 50mm

The mesh system 7m PVA Hydrospol is used in various situations when casting or baiting.

All of our stockings are made using only high quality Japanese fibers in the form of a dense micro-mesh knitting system. A spun fibre that allows the pva material to dissolve more slowly. Our stockings can be filled with loose materials or small pellets. Thanks to our petting, your blend will not sag out. The strong thread and knitting method of the PVA stocking can also cope with sharp and far casting.

The dissolving time depends on the water temperature. In warm water the dissolution time is around 45 seconds, on the other hand in ice water it can be up to 8 minutes.

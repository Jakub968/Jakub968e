PVA MESH

PVA Hydrospol offers a wide range of PVA Mesh in various diameters and lengths, including versions with different dissolution rates. These meshes are made from polyvinyl alcohol (PVA), which gradually dissolves upon contact with water, making them ideal for environmentally friendly fishing, particularly for carp anglers.

Types and Features of PVA Mesh:
1. Slow-dissolving PVA Mesh – These meshes are ideal for deeper or colder waters, where slower dissolution is required to ensure the bait remains in place for an extended period. This type is preferred in situations where the bait needs to last longer before fully dissolving.

2. Diameters and Lengths – PVA Hydrospol offers mesh in various diameters:
- 15 mm: This smaller diameter is suitable for a more delicate bait presentation or smaller boilies that need compact packing.
- 25 mm and 35 mm: These sizes are ideal for standard-sized boilies and loose bait.
- 50 mm: The larger diameter is suited for bulkier bait or situations where more bait needs to be packed into the mesh.

Regarding lengths, PVA Mesh come in a range of sizes, including 7 meters for shorter sessions and up to 25 meters for more intensive fishing trips.

Premium Quality Materials:
PVA Hydrospol is renowned for using high-quality Japanese spun fibers, which are woven at micro-density. This advanced material ensures durability and a more precise dissolution rate, making it a top choice for anglers looking for reliability and consistency in their fishing gear.

2-in-1 System – 7 + 7 Meters:
One of their popular products is the PVA 2-in-1 system, which includes 7 meters of fast-dissolving mesh and 7 meters of slow-dissolving mesh. This system allows anglers to adjust to different conditions – the fast-dissolving mesh is ideal for shallower or warmer waters, while the slow-dissolving version is better for deeper or colder waters.

Replacements and Accessories:
PVA Hydrospol also provides replacement PVA Mesh, available as refills for existing systems. These refills come in all standard diameters, including 15 mm, 25 mm, 35 mm, and 50 mm, allowing anglers to replenish their supplies without purchasing new applicators. The use of Japanese micro-spun fibers in these meshes ensures the highest quality and performance in a variety of fishing conditions.

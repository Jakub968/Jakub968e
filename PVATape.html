PVA TAPE

PVA Hydrospol offers a range of specialized products for fishing, including PVA tape, PVA thread, and an extra strong version of PVA thread.

Contact Manufacturing

PVA Tape

diameter 16mm

### 1. **PVA Tape 16mm (45-micron thickness)**:
- This **16mm-wide PVA tape** is a versatile tool for securing bait when using PVA bags or stockings. Its **45-micron thickness** ensures durability while still allowing for rapid dissolution in water. This makes it ideal for binding boilies or securing other bait types in PVA bags before casting. The tape dissolves quickly, making it effective for releasing bait in both warm and cooler waters. It's a great choice for anglers who need precision in bait placement without worrying about delayed release.

### 2. **PVA Thread**:
- **PVA thread** is used for tying off or securing bait bundles, such as boilies or pellets, when placing them inside PVA bags or stockings. This thread is designed to dissolve completely in water, allowing for precise bait release without leaving any residue. It is known for its ease of use and adaptability to different fishing styles and bait sizes. The thread is thin yet strong enough to hold the bait securely until it is submerged in water.

### 3. **PVA Thread Extra Strong**:
- The **PVA Extra Strong Thread** is a more robust version of the standard PVA thread, designed for situations that require added durability and strength. It is ideal for securing larger or heavier bait setups, particularly in deeper or flowing waters where additional strength is needed. Despite its extra strength, it still dissolves efficiently upon contact with water, ensuring timely bait release. This product is favored by anglers who need reliable performance in more challenging fishing environments.

These products are crafted from high-quality materials, ensuring that they meet the needs of anglers in a variety of fishing conditions, from shallow waters to deeper, colder environments.

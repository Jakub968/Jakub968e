PVA BAGS

PVA Hydrospol offers PVA bags made from high-quality English PVA film. These bags are highly regarded for their 40-micron thickness, which provides durability while still ensuring rapid and efficient dissolution in water. The film’s thickness allows the bags to withstand handling, yet they dissolve quickly upon contact with water, making them perfect for precise bait delivery during carp fishing.

Contact Manufacturing

PVA Bags

diameter 15mm - 50mm

Key Features:
1. English PVA Film: Known for its quality, this film ensures reliable performance in various water conditions. It’s 40-micron thickness provides a balance between strength and fast dissolution, giving anglers confidence that their bait will be released at the right moment.

2. Ideal Dissolution: These PVA bags dissolve efficiently in water, even in colder temperatures, making them suitable for use in diverse fishing environments. This ensures that the bait is delivered precisely where it is needed, without leaving residue.

3. Available Sizes:
- 70 x 100 mm: Ideal for smaller bait setups or when precision is required in bait placement.
- 70 x 200 mm: This size offers a longer, more slender bag for slightly larger bait loads.
- 100 x 130 mm: A larger option, ideal for bulkier bait or when a bigger bait parcel is needed.

Each pack includes a scoop for easy and fast filling of the bags, making it much more convenient to handle the bait on-site.

These bags are designed to be versatile and effective, providing anglers with the right balance of size and dissolution time for different fishing strategies and water conditions.

HACK

Cutter Boilies

For boilies with a diameter
from 20 to 25 mm

The PVA Hydrospol Hack is a versatile tool designed for cutting boilies, making it ideal for anglers when preparing bait. It is suited for boilies with a diameter of 20 to 25 mm, allowing for quick and effortless cutting with a single press. This tool is highly valued for its simplicity and improved ergonomics, offering comfortable handling.

Compared to the original version, which was made using 3D printing, the new **Hack** is injection molded and crafted from durable plastic, significantly increasing its lifespan. One of its key features is the ability to replace the blades, which removes the need to purchase an entirely new tool when the blades wear out. The sharp blades easily cut through even hard boilies, simplifying bait preparation.

The tool is lightweight and compact, making it easy to store in a small pouch. The package includes replacement blades, which can be easily purchased at standard hardware stores. The PVA Hydrospol Hack is a favorite for its efficiency and simplicity, helping anglers save time when preparing bait for fishing.
